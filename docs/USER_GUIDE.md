## Getting Started

### First Time Login
1. **Access the Platform** — Navigate to the app URL and enter your email and password provided by your administrator.
2. **Complete Your Profile** — Click on your avatar in the top right corner, select "Profile Settings", and fill in your information (Full Name, Job Title, Phone Number, Profile Picture).
3. **Set Your Daily Goals** — Go to Dashboard, review your daily Power Moves, and set personal targets for contacts touched per day, activities logged, and weekly loan volume goals.

## Dashboard Overview

Your dashboard is your command center. It displays:

### Key Metrics
- **Momentum Score**: Your overall performance score (0–1000)
- **Current Streak**: Consecutive days completing Power Moves
- **Weekly Points**: Total points earned this week
- **Active Contacts**: Number of engaged contacts

### Quick Actions
- Log new activity
- Add new contact
- View today's Power Moves
- Check AI recommendations

### Widgets
- **AI Coach**: Personalized insights and recommendations
- **Quick Stats**: Performance snapshot
- **Upcoming Meetings**: From calendar integration
- **Recent Activities**: Latest logged actions

---

## Daily Power Moves

Power Moves are your daily objectives designed to drive consistent performance.

### Default Power Moves (customize in settings):
1. Log 5+ contact touchpoints
2. Make 3+ phone calls
3. Send 5+ personalized emails
4. Update 3+ contact records
5. Review and respond to all inquiries

### Completing Power Moves
1. Navigate to Dashboard
2. Review assigned moves for the day
3. Complete each move and mark it done
4. Track your daily grade (A–F)

## Contact Management

### Adding Contacts
1. Navigate to **Contacts** from the sidebar
2. Click **Add Contact**
3. Fill in name, email, phone, company, and contact type
4. Save the contact

### Contact Health Scores
Each contact has a health score (0–100) based on:
- Recency of last interaction
- Total number of touches
- Loans closed

### Health Status Tiers
| Score | Status |
|-------|--------|
| 80–100 | Excellent |
| 60–79 | Good |
| 40–59 | Neutral |
| 20–39 | At Risk |
| 0–19 | Critical |

## Activity Tracking

### Logging Activities
1. Navigate to **Activities** from the sidebar
2. Click **Log Activity**
3. Select activity type (Call, Email, Meeting, etc.)
4. Associate with a contact (optional)
5. Add description and submit

### Points System
- Calls: 10 points
- Emails: 5 points
- Meetings: 15 points
- Referrals: 20 points

## Email Integration

### Connecting Outlook

2. Click **Connect Outlook**
3. Sign in with your Microsoft account
4. Grant requested permissions

- Sync emails automatically
- Track email activity with contacts
- Use email templates

## AI Insights

The AI Coach provides personalized recommendations based on your activity patterns.

### Features
- **Performance Alerts**: Notifications when metrics drop
- **Contact Recommendations**: Suggestions on who to reach out to
- **Weekly Summaries**: Automated performance digests
- **At-Risk Contact Alerts**: Early warnings for contacts needing attention

## Email Automation

### Creating Sequences
1. Navigate to **Integrations > Email Sequences**
2. Define trigger conditions
3. Add sequence steps with delays
4. Activate the sequence

### Enrollment
Contacts matching trigger conditions are automatically enrolled in sequences.

## Leaderboard & Gamification

### Viewing the Leaderboard
1. Navigate to **Leaderboard** from the sidebar
2. Toggle between Daily, Weekly, Monthly views
3. View rankings by points, volume, or loans

### Tiers
| Points | Tier |
|--------|------|
| 800+ | Diamond |
| 600–799 | Platinum |
| 400–599 | Gold |
| 200–399 | Silver |
| 0–199 | Bronze |

### Achievements
Earn badges for milestones like streak counts, activity volumes, and loan closings.
